So Why Me?


I am an excellent learner
Whether by instruction or independent effort, I will learn whatever is needed to succeed. I am equally capable with both methods, and self-study has become a strength of mine due to taking online courses and preparing for the CPA exam. 

I follow the motto, "Measure thrice, cut once"
My work is thorough and deliberate. I undertake careful preparations and never do anything haphazardly. Even under the pressure of deadlines, I maintain a high level of work quality and performance. 

Constraints bring out my creativity and resourcefulness
I am able to make do and even thrive in such environments. Instead of thinking, "This can't be done," I go straight to the issue of "How can this be achieved given the available resources?" 

I know when to ask for help
Usually I will try to find answers on my own. But in time-sensitive and other special situations, I will consult with superiors and colleagues. 

Problem-solving is always on my mind
From financial and technical to everyday issues, I am constantly thinking of potential improvements, solutions and workarounds.
